Add Products to Lightbox: Single Product & Bulk

Step 1: Login to your Amplifi Account

Visit uxwa.amplifi.io/portal and login to your Amplifi account. If you haven't received an Amplifi invitation please reach out to your contact at Umarex USA and one will be sent to you.

Step 2: Search for your product(s)

There are multiple ways to find the product you're looking for. You can search the name or SKU using the search bar, select one of our featured folders or search through the our categories by selecting the 'Categories' menu in the top left.

Step 3: Add One (1) Product to your Lightbox

Hover over the product you wish to add and click the 'Add to Lightbox' icon.

Step 3: Add Multiple Products to your Lightbox

Follow the previous steps of searching for your products. Once you've found them just click the check box in the upper right corner of the products you wish to add to your Lightbox.

Click 'Batch Actions' and then select 'Add to Lightbox' from the dropdown.

Step 4: Create or Select your Lightbox

Either select a previously created lightbox from the drop down or create a new one. Once you've done that just hit 'Save & Close'.

You should see a popup stating that the assets were successfully added to the Lightbox

Step 5:

To access your Lightbox just click 'Select Lightbox' in the top right corner and select your Lightbox from the list. It'll then show your Lightbox content in a panel on the right side.

Download Your Data

Step 1: Download Images

To download images for your selected products click the 'Download' button from within the Lightbox panel. You'll get a pop up for 'Download Preferences for Lightbox'. We recommend selecting 'Original' and then you can resize as needed, but you are free to select any of the options it gives you. Once you've selected your preferences just hit 'Download'.

You will receive an email with a link to download all of your assets.

Once downloaded you'll have a folder similar to this. You can disregard the Excel sheets in the folders.

Step 2: Download Product Information

To download product info click the 'Generate Lightbox Data' from within the Lightbox panel. Leave the Export Template as 'UX Export Template' and then choose whether between 'CSV' or 'XLSX' and then click 'Continue'. This will download it straight to your 'Downloads' folder on your computer.
